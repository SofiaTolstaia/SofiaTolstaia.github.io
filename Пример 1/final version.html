A Revolutionary New Tool For Mapping Brain Health

At elminda, we envision a world in which brain healthcare is intelligent, data-driven and available to everyone.

Integrating big-data repositories and deep-learning algorithms, our state-of-the-art BNA™ (Brain Network Analytics) technology holds the potential to revolutionize the diagnosis, monitoring and treatment of brain-related disorders by measuring how effectively different parts of the brain are connecting and communicating*.

Better Decision-Making for Physicians and Patients

BNA allows medical professionals –to see more and know more than ever before.

*At present the insights generated by the BNA system are mainly mapping neural networks.
